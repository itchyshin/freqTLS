# Legacy notice: brown-shrimp compatibility fixture

The brown-shrimp objects are unpublished compatibility fixtures retained
for internal benchmark continuity. They are not an active freqTLS
teaching example and are intentionally absent from case-study
navigation, current comparison tables, and discovery indexes.

Use the canonical empirical sequence instead: oxygen-gradient zebrafish,
cereal aphids, Snow-gum PSII, and the mortality and awake/coma
*Drosophila suzukii* examples. The historical data objects have not been
deleted or deprecated in this phase.
