# Figure audit: experimental v0.2 pkgdown

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Scope:** all 12 figures produced by a fresh `Rscript tools/build-site.R` build
of the v0.2 closure tree. Each PNG was opened and inspected directly; no verdict
is based only on source code or a contact sheet.

## Pattern checks

- `rg "posterior|credible" R vignettes README.Rmd docs` returned deliberate
  bayesTLS contrasts, prohibitions, or historical audit material; no freqTLS
  figure is described as posterior or credible.
- The three Confidence-Eye surfaces use pale lenses, darker outlines, and hollow
  point estimates. The open-profile surface draws no lens.
- Every interval figure names the method and scale in its title, subtitle, or
  caption. Point-only fitted-curve figures do not imply uncertainty bands.
- The new site-warning layout does not alter any figure recipe. Desktop page
  screenshots show the warning, article heading, and figures in normal document
  flow below the fixed navbar.

## Verdict

All 12 current article figures pass the v0.2 visual gate. The Snow-gum and
zebrafish empirical Confidence Eyes use Wald intervals and say so; the synthetic
intro eye uses profile likelihood; the sparse-design example refuses to draw a
closed eye. No figure change is required in the closure PR.
